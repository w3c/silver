Silver Prototype Example - Flavors

The purpose of this prototype is to show how it may be possible to include a variety of different flavors into silver. A flavor is a particular implementation of silver guidance to a given technology.

In this prototype we take Success Criteria 1.1.1 - Non Text Content from WCAG 2.0. At this early stage, no IA has been constructed for Silver so prototype creation may use existing IA from WCAG as inspiration for prototype development. The content is just to give you an idea (it's better than lorem ipsum). There is no content developed yet for Silver. Please don't comment on the actual text of the content.

Styling information on the different flavors is available in this page.
